Blinding Tuft (Thief) is ★★★★★, not low priority. Skull Fear (Necromancer) is ★★★★, excellent for interrupt + fear. Check the table before writing any stolen skill assessment.

## DO NOT HIT REFERENCE TABLE

These skills punish attackers. If the player hits into them, bad things happen. Always include relevant entries in the "DO NOT HIT" section of advice, filtered to only skills present in the current enemy composition.

| Skill | Profession | What Happens | Correct Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| Defiant Stance | Warrior | Absorbs ALL incoming damage → converts to healing | Stop attacking. Wait 3-4 seconds. Resume when glow fades. |
| Endure Pain | Warrior | Immune to strike (direct) damage for 3s. Conditions still work. | Apply conditions if possible, otherwise wait it out. |
| Renewed Focus | Guardian | Invulnerable for 3 seconds. Rooted in place. Refreshes all virtues. | Count to 3, then burst immediately. They can't move during it. |
| Aegis (boon) | Guardian | Blocks exactly ONE attack, then gone. | Pop with a throwaway hit (auto attack, Dancing Dagger, SB1) BEFORE committing burst. |
| Obsidian Flesh | Elementalist (Earth 5 Dagger) | Invulnerable but rooted. 4 second channel. | Reposition for advantage, wait it out. |
| Hare's Agility / blur | Evoker | Invulnerable AND can still cast skills. | Disengage entirely. Re-engage after ~5 seconds. |
| Stone Heart | Elementalist (Earth attunement trait) | Cannot go below 1 HP while in Earth attunement. | STOP ATTACKING. Wait for them to swap attunements, THEN burst. |
| Reaper Shroud | Necromancer (Reaper) | Damage hits shroud HP, not real HP. Effectively a second health bar. | Drain shroud first with poke damage, then burst when shroud drops. |
| Distortion (F4 shatter) | Mesmer | Brief invulnerability. | Wait ~1 second, then burst. Very short duration. |
| Magnetic Shield (Shield 4) | Engineer | Reflects ALL projectiles back at you. | Do not use ranged attacks. Melee or wait. |
| Full Counter | Warrior (Spellbreaker) | Blocks the next attack, then counter-attacks with a daze. | Bait with auto attack. Do NOT commit burst skills into it. |
| Shocking Aura | Anyone (shared by Tempest) | YOU get stunned for hitting THEM. | Avoid melee contact. Strip the aura or wait for it to expire (~4s). |

## ROLE DEFINITIONS

| Role | Description | Typical Behavior |
|---|---|---|
| **Roamer** | Fast rotation between capture points. +1 fights (turning 1v1s into 2v1s). Decap and leave. Never sits on a node longer than 10 seconds. | Arrives at fights, bursts a low target, leaves. Constantly moving. |
| **Duelist** | Holds side nodes in 1v1 combat. Wins extended fights. The point is their job. | Sits on far or close, fights whoever contests. Calls for help when outnumbered. |
| **Support** | Stays with the team. Heals and buffs. Never chases kills. Teammates die without them nearby. | Glued to their carry or the teamfight ball. Peels for backline when dived. |
| **Teamfighter** | Strongest in group fights. Wins mid brawls. Cleaves downed enemies. | Groups mid, fights with team, uses AoE pressure. |
| **Bunker** | Sits on a capture point. Nearly unkillable. Stalls enemies and buys time. Not worth fighting 1v1. | Stands on point. Outlasts. Forces enemy to send 2+ players or give up the point. |

## TEAM COMPOSITION ANALYSIS RULES

Use these to assess the enemy team and advise accordingly:

- **0 enemy supports** → damage sticks permanently. Play aggressive. Every fight is winnable.
- **1 enemy support** → either focus the support in teamfights to remove their healing, OR catch DPS away from the support and burst them down.
- **2 enemy supports** → do NOT teamfight. You will lose sustained engagements. Split the map, decap aggressively, force them to spread out.
- **Multiple enemy roamers** → watch your back when decapping. They will hunt you. Travel with a buddy or avoid isolated fights.
- **No enemy roamer** → free rotations. Abuse your mobility advantage, decap constantly.
- **Mirror matchup** (same spec on both teams) → note it explicitly. Skill diff decides.
- **Heavy CC comp** → save stunbreaks, don't blow them early. Call out CC chains in advice.
- **Heavy boon comp** (Herald + Firebrand etc.) → boon strip is critical before every engage.
- **Heavy condition comp** → condi cleanse timing matters. Mention specific cleanse cooldowns.

## MAP STRATEGIES

### Conquest (Generic 3-Point)
- Standard rotation: rush far → decap → rotate mid → +1 → repeat
- Core rule: don't fullcap far. Decap (neutralize) and leave. Fullcap close is fine.
- Never sit on a point longer than 10 seconds. Constant movement.
- Check minimap every 5 seconds.

### Legacy of the Foefire
- Same as standard Conquest + Lord room mechanic.
- Lord room push: viable at 100+ point lead. Coordinate with team.
- Lord has significant HP — needs multiple players to kill.
- Thieves can snipe lord if enemy team is wiped or distracted.
- Watch for enemy lord rushes when you're ahead.

### Battle of Kyhlo
- Trebuchets on each side can destroy capture point structures and do massive AoE.
- Thief/roamer job: destroy enemy trebuchet (it's vulnerable, low HP).
- Clocktower provides verticality — abuse it for ambushes and LoS.
- Treb shots are telegraphed — move when you see the targeting circle.

### Temple of the Silent Storm
- **Stillness** (side objective): doubles node income for capturing team. Channel to capture.
- **Tranquility** (center): instantly captures all 3 nodes for your team. Very powerful.
- Deny channels with interrupts (Steal, CC). Bodyguard teammates channeling.
- Tranquility spawns periodically — time your rotations around it.

### Forest of Niflhel
- Two neutral bosses: **Svanir** (northeast) and **Chieftain** (southwest).
- Bosses are **present from match start** — they do NOT spawn on a timer. They respawn ~3 minutes after being killed.
- Killing a boss: **25 points + Aura of Power** (+50 all stats, 90 seconds). Last hit gets credit.
- Boss stealing (last-hitting with burst) is a viable thief tactic. Steal (F1) interrupts enemy boss channels.
- Don't solo bosses unless safe — they hit hard and enemies will contest.
- Mid is the primary teamfight area. +1 mid aggressively.
- **Map opening**: rush far node → contest/decap → rotate to nearest boss immediately after. Bosses are already up from the start — don't wait.

### Eternal Coliseum
- **Sword of Reaping** spawns mid. Carrier gets AoE damage + special skills.
- **Shield buffs** also available.
- Let tankier classes grab the sword. Roamers/squishies shouldn't carry it.
- Otherwise plays like standard Conquest.

### Revenge of the Capricorn
- **Bell** spawns periodically as a temporary 4th capture point.
- Bell awards escalating bonus points each time it's captured.
- Fast-rotating classes should contest bell every time it spawns.
- Otherwise standard 3-point Conquest.

### Skyhammer
- **Skyhammer cannon** platform in center. Fires a devastating AoE that neutralizes nodes.
- Huge map — mobility is king. Slow classes struggle with rotations.
- Contest the skyhammer platform every ~3 minutes when it activates.
- Map favors roamers and mobile classes.

### Djinn's Dominion
- **Sandstorm** periodically sweeps across mid, blocking LoS.
- Sandstorm changes rotation timing — you can cross mid unseen, or lose target during a fight.
- Plan engagements around sandstorm timing.
- Otherwise standard Conquest.

### Spirit Watch
- **Orb of Ascension** spawns mid. Carry it to a friendly capture point for bonus points (50 to friendly, 25 to enemy + neutralizes).
- DON'T carry the orb as a squishy class — it removes stealth, swiftness, and weapon skills, and slows you by 40%.
- Thief job: deny enemy orb pickups (Steal interrupts the channel), punish orb carriers (slow sitting ducks), escort allied orb carriers.
- Teleporting drops the orb.

### Sunjiang Backstreets (Push Mode)
- Push mode: contest a central point that moves toward the losing team's base.
- Jade Consoles and Cannon Controllers are side objectives.
- Roamers: grab side objectives (fastest to them), then +1 point fights.
- Don't overcommit to point — be the mosquito.

### Battle of Champion's Dusk (Stronghold Mode)
- Supply system: pick up supply (2 max), spend at barracks to summon doorbreakers/archers.
- Mist Champions can be summoned — powerful NPCs. Channel to summon.
- Thief role: supply runner (fastest class), guard killer, Mist Champion channel interrupter.
- Supply pile: 2s channel, refills 15s after emptied. Killed players drop 1 supply.
